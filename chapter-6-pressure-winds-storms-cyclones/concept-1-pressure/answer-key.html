Section B Short answer

Q8

Section C Long / application

Q11
(i) The pointed end has a tiny area, so it gives very high pressure for the applied force and pierces easily. (ii) Wide tyres spread the tractor's weight over a large area, lowering the pressure on soft soil so it doesn't sink.
Q12
