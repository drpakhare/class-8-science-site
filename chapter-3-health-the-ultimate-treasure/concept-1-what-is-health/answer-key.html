Section B Short answer

Q8
Health = complete physical, mental and social well-being, not merely the absence of disease. Aspects: physical, mental, social.
Q9
A symptom is what the patient feels (e.g. pain); a sign is what can be seen or measured (e.g. fever/rash).
Q10
Balanced diet, regular exercise, enough sleep, limiting screen time, personal hygiene, yoga, saying no to tobacco/alcohol (any four).

Section C Long / application

Q11
His social health suffered (lonely, no friends), affecting his mental health (sad, sleepless) and then his physical health (weight loss, headaches). Steps: reduce screen time, meet a counsellor, make friends, exercise and keep a routine (any two).
Q12
Clean air prevents breathing problems like coughing/asthma (polluted city air harms lungs); clean water and surroundings prevent disease-spreading flies/mosquitoes, so people fall sick less often.
